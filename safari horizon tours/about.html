Our Story

Safari Horizon Tours was founded with a passion for wildlife and a commitment to providing unforgettable safari experiences. Our journey began with a group of avid travelers and wildlife enthusiasts who wanted to share the beauty of Africa's wilderness with the world. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name in the safari industry, known for our dedication to conservation, exceptional service, and unforgettable adventures.

Our Core Values

  • Conservation: We are committed to preserving the natural habitats and wildlife of Africa. We work closely with local communities and conservation organizations to ensure that our tours have a positive impact on the environment.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We strive to provide exceptional service and unforgettable experiences that exceed our customers' expectations.
  • Authenticity: We believe in providing authentic safari experiences that allow our customers to connect with nature and wildlife in a meaningful way.
  • Safety: The safety of our customers is our top priority. We adhere to strict safety protocols and employ experienced guides to ensure a safe and enjoyable safari experience.
